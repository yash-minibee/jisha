About Jisha Polyfilm

Excellence in Plastic Manufacturing

Who We Are

Jisha Polyfilm is a trusted supplier of LDPE shrink films, liner bags, courier bag rolls, mulching film rolls, and 3 layer films for industrial packaging solutions. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for businesses across various sectors.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for companies seeking premium plastic solutions. We combine advanced manufacturing techniques with stringent quality control to deliver products that meet the highest industry standards.

Our Mission

To provide reliable plastic solutions with excellent quality and timely delivery. We strive to exceed customer expectations through continuous improvement and innovation in our manufacturing processes.

Our Vision

To become a trusted name in the plastic and packaging material industry. We aim to be recognized for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer-centric approach in everything we do.
